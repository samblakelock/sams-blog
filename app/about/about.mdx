**Sam** is the co-founder and CEO of [Pickup Music](https://pickupmusic.com), an independent education technology company based in Los Angeles.

In 2015, he founded Pickup Music while studying jazz guitar in New York City. From 2015-2018, he grew Pickup Music to become the leading online community of musicians with over a million Instagram followers. It then evolved into the leading provider of music education with over 20,000 members.

Sam's music has been streamed millions of times. His work has been featured on GuitarWorld.com and in Total Guitar magazine. He has performed at the Blue Note Jazz Club, Smalls Jazz Club, and Brooklyn Bowl.

At Pickup Music, Sam leads the content, curriculum, and engineering teams.
